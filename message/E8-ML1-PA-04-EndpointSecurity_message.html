E8 ML1-PA-04 - Endpoint Security check - weekly
A vulnerability scanner is used at least weekly to identify missing patches or updates for vulnerabilities in office productivity suites, web browsers and their extensions, email clients, PDF software, and security products.

Devices listed below were identified as having issues with Defender and/or Defender Firewall. Configuration items checked are:

  1. DefenderEnabled: '0' indicates Defender is disabled.
  2. DefenderUpToDate: '0' indicates Defender is not up to date.
  3. WindowsDefenderFirewallEnabled - '1' indicates Firewall is enabled.
  4. ActiveProfile - 'Unknown' indicates that there is no active profile, Firewall is effectively disabled.
Further details available in the Defender portal. Close this ticket only after ensuring Defender updates are applying and/or Defender Firewall is enabled and the appropriate network profile is enabled e.g. 'Domain' for AD joined devices
